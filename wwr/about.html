Northwest Trailblazers

At Northwest Trailblazers, we are committed to delivering exciting and dependable whitewater rafting experiences. Our trained guides prioritize safety, professionalism, and respect for the river while ensuring every trip is memorable and well-led.

History

Northwest Trailblazers was founded in 2014 in North Idaho by a small group of experienced river runners who had spent years exploring the region's waterways. Starting with a single raft and a commitment to sharing local rivers, the company grew steadily through word-of-mouth and a strong reputation for safety and professionalism. Over time, Northwest Trailblazers expanded its fleet, trained certified guides, and established trips on multiple rivers across the Northwest. Despite this growth, the company continues to operate with its original focus: skilled guidance, environmental stewardship, and consistent, high-quality rafting experiences.
